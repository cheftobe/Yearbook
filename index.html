About Me

I'm 17 year old junior who attends Food and Finance High School, and Harlem School of the Arts. I've been at FFHS 2 years now, where I have the opportunity to become a “Modern chef” and build my brand Cheftobe. I've attended HSA for 4 years now (3 years as college prep) and have used my skills acquired there to build my brand Gloabalarts. Similar to my Cheftobe platform will connect artist of all kinds for inspiration and a place where my creations can be sold, such handmade keychains, necklaces, and more.

I enjoy baking, mostly because it is a way to express myself and add my artistic visions of life to food. I run a part time catering business that i want to turn into my Pâtisserie. , where i will teach the next generation and sell freshly baked goods the echo peoples favorite childhood desserts and savory dishes.
